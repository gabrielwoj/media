This section contains sprays that can be used in Team Fortress 2, or most Source games. Clicking on the preview will enlarge the image and show the spray in full size.
To download a spray, you can click on the download button located at the top-right corner of the screen (when previewing).
To use these Team Fortress 2, you must save the spray anywhere on your computer. Launch TF2, go to Options -> Multiplayer -> Import Spray. A folder explorer will show up. Navigate through the folders and select the spray on the location you've originally saved it, and then, press the Open button.
