This section contains customized icons for the Windows Taskbar (or other places such as the Desktop). Clicking on an icon will enlarge the image and show a bigger preview for the icon.
To download the icon in .ico format, you can click on the download button located at the top-right corner of the screen (when previewing).
Each icon has different available icon sizes, however, all of them work for the Taskbar while having the "Use small icons" option enabled.
